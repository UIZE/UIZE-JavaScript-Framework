2009 NEWS 2009-01-21 - IMPROVED EXAMPLE: ImageWipe

The ImageWipe example has been improved in a number of ways.

A new tabbed interface provides you with a "PRESETS" tab for previewing the wide variety of wipe effect presets available, and a "PARAMS" tab for experimenting with the under-the-hood settings for the image wipe effect. Additionally, a whole bunch of new presets recently added to the new Uize.Widget.ImageWipe.xPresets extension module are now available in the "PRESETS" tab.
