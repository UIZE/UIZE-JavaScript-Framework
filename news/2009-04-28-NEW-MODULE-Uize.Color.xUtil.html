2009 NEWS 2009-04-28 - NEW MODULE: Uize.Color.xUtil

The new Uize.Color.xUtil extension module extends the Uize.Color object by adding instance and static methods for mixing, blending, comparing, sorting, and otherwise working with colors.

Using the Uize.Color.xUtil module, combination colors can be generated, arrays of colors can be sorted by their proximity to a reference color, two colors can be blended in varying degrees, colors can be randomized in their color space, two colors can be tested for equality, and an array of colors can be mixed together in equal amounts to create an average color.
