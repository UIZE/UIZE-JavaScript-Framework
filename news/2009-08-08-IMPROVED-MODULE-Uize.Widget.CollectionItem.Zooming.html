2009 NEWS 2009-08-08 - IMPROVED MODULE: Uize.Widget.CollectionItem.Zooming

The Uize.Widget.CollectionItem.Zooming module has been improved with the addition of the new deadMargin and zoomFadeProperties set-get properties.

The new deadMargin set-get property lets you configure the thickness of the margin at the edges of the preview image where moving the mouse will produce no change in the values of either - or both - of the alignX and alignY set-get properties. The new zoomFadeProperties set-get property lets you specify properties that should be used for the Uize.Fade instance that drives the zoom in effect's animation.
