2009 NEWS 2009-08-08 - IMPROVED MODULE: Uize.Widget.CollectionItem.Zooming

The Uize.Widget.CollectionItem.Zooming module has been improved with the addition of the new deadMargin and zoomFadeProperties state properties.

The new deadMargin state property lets you configure the thickness of the margin at the edges of the preview image where moving the mouse will produce no change in the values of either - or both - of the alignX and alignY state properties. The new zoomFadeProperties state property lets you specify properties that should be used for the Uize.Fade instance that drives the zoom in effect's animation.
