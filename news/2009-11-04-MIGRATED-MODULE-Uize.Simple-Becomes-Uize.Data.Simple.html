2009 NEWS 2009-11-04 - MIGRATED MODULE: Uize.Simple Becomes Uize.Data.Simple

The Uize.Simple module has been migrated to under the Uize.Data namespace to become Uize.Data.Simple, to get it out of the root Uize namespace.

This change is not backwards compatible, but is likely to affect only a very limited number of developers (probably not you). If you are using this module (unlikely), then you will have to change your code to reflect this change.
