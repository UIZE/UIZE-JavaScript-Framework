2009 NEWS 2009-02-14 - NEW MODULE: Uize.Widget.Swap.xPresets

A plethora of effect presets for the Uize.Widget.Swap class have been made available in the new Uize.Widget.Swap.xPresets extension module.

Presets in this new module include: crossFade, fadeOutFadeIn, fadeOutPauseThenFadeIn, fadeWipeFromLeft, fadeWipeFromRight, fadeWipeHorizontalFromCenter, fadeWipeFromTop, fadeWipeFromBottom, fadeWipeVerticalFromCenter, fadeSlideInFromLeft, fadeSlideInFromRight, fadeSlideInFromTop, fadeSlideInFromBottom, fadeSlideInFromTopLeft, fadeSlideInFromTopRight, fadeSlideInFromBottomLeft, fadeSlideInFromBottomRight, fadeSlideOutSlideInLeft, fadeSlideOutSlideInRight, fadeSlideOutSlideInTop, fadeSlideOutSlideInBottom, fadeSlideOutSlideInTopLeft, fadeSlideOutSlideInTopRight, fadeSlideOutSlideInBottomLeft, fadeSlideOutSlideInBottomRight, wipeOutWipeInLeft, wipeOutWipeInRight, wipeOutWipeInTop, wipeOutWipeInBottom, wipeOutWipeInTopLeft, wipeOutWipeInTopRight, wipeOutWipeInBottomLeft, wipeOutWipeInBottomRight, wipeOutWipeInHorizontalCenter, wipeOutWipeInVerticalCenter, horizontalCrossSlideFromLeft, horizontalCrossSlideFromRight, verticalCrossSlideFromTop, verticalCrossSlideFromBottom, closeToCenterOpenFromCenter, poofPauseThenOpenFromCenter, and centerOpenCloseCrossFade.

The presets can be previewed and tweaked in the new Uize.Widget.Swap.Image example. Full documentation and a description of all the presets is also available.
