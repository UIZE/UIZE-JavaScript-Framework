2008 NEWS 2008-07-01 - NEW WIDGET: Uize.Widget.AutoTooltip

Another "unobtrusive JavaScript" feature has been added to the UIZE JavaScript Framework, in the form of the new Uize.Widget.AutoTooltip class.

This widget lets you easily wire an auto tooltip behavior to a specified array of nodes, to provide decorated / fancy tooltips in a way that degrades gracefully.

Check out the new Auto Tooltip for Photo Info example that demonstrates this new functionality.
