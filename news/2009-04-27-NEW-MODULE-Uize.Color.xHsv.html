2009 NEWS 2009-04-27 - NEW MODULE: Uize.Color.xHsv

The new Uize.Color.xHsv extension module extends the Uize.Color object by defining the HSV (Hue, Saturation, Value) color space.

Similar to the built-in HSL color space, the HSV color space also expresses colors in terms of their hue on a 360 degree color wheel. There are subtle differences between how grayness and lightness / darkness are expressed through the saturation and value components of HSV, and the saturation and lightness components of HSL.
