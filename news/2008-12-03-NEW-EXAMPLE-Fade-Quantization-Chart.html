2008 NEWS 2008-12-03 - NEW EXAMPLE: Fade Quantization Chart

The new Fade Quantization Chart example illustrates, by way of a color chart representation, how the quantization state property of the Uize.Fade class affects a fade's interpolated value over the course of its progress.

In the example, a color is being faded from red to black over a series of swatches, from left to right. The color chart lets you visualize how different quantization settings for a fade could be used to generate color gradients with varying characteristics.
