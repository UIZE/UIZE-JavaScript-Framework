2009 NEWS 2009-02-15 - NEW EXAMPLE: Uize.Widget.Swap.Image

The new Uize.Widget.Swap.Image example demonstrates the image swap effect implemented in the Uize.Widget.Swap.Image class, as well as the many effect presets available in the Uize.Widget.Swap.xPresets extension module.

A tabbed interface provides you with a "PRESETS" tab for previewing the wide variety of swap effect presets available, and a "PARAMS" tab for experimenting with the under-the-hood settings for the image swap effect.
