2009 NEWS 2009-05-11 - NEW EXAMPLE: Deck Swapper

The new Deck Swapper example demonstrates use of the Uize.Widget.Swap.Deck widget class.

In the example, an instance of the Uize.Widget.Swap.Deck class is wired up to a deck of divs in the document in order to provide a transition effect when swapping from one item in the deck to another. Making the widget switch to another item with an accompanying transition effect is as simple as setting the widget's itemNo set-get property.
