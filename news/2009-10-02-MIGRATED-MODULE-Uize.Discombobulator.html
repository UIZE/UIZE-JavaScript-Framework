2009 NEWS 2009-10-02 - MIGRATED MODULE: Uize.Discombobulator

The Uize.Discombobulator module, with its somewhat bizarre, esoteric, and undocumented string munging functionality, has been migrated to under the Uize.String namespace, as Uize.String.Discombobulator, to get it out of the root Uize namespace.

This change is not backwards compatible. The single user of this module (you know who you are) will have to change his code to use the new module name. Sorry (he says, apologizing to himself).
