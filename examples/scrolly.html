JAVASCRIPT EXAMPLES Scrolly
SOURCE

In this example, two instances of Uize.Widget.Scrolly are created - one for a horizontal scrolly, and the other for a vertical scrolly. Click on the scrolly navigation buttons to scroll a scrolly's view node, one "page" at a time, with an accompanying JavaScript animation effect. Shift-click on the navigation buttons to scroll all the way to the beginning or end. Also, because this example uses the Uize.Widget.ThumbZoom class, you can click on the thumbnails to get a zoom out preview.
